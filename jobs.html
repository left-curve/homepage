Frontend Lead

[+]

You will lead the development of an innovative "portal" webapp, which we believe will drastically change the way users interact with blockchains. This portal will have two characteristics:

  • A "super app" experience. Instead of navigating many different domains and websites, users will access all apps on our chain through a single portal app. Apps are abstracted as "applets" which are loaded into the DOM upon user request. We will create a design guideline and an SDK to faciliate development of applets.
  • Key-less wallet. Instead of figuring out seed phrases and browser extensions, users will generate and store Passkeys in their device or clour keychains, and sign transactions using fingerprint or Face ID.

We want to make the app available both in broswer as well as iOS and Android apps, so familiarity with technologies such as React Native is preferred.

Compensation: cash + equity + tokens.
